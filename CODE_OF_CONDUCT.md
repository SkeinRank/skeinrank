# Code of Conduct

SkeinRank aims to be a professional and welcoming open-source project for search, data, platform, and AI infrastructure engineers.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while being precise about technical risks.
- Keep bug reports actionable and reproducible.
- Avoid sharing private company data, credentials, logs, or customer information.
- Disagree with ideas and designs, not with people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing someone else's private information.
- Sharing secrets or exploit details in public issues.
- Repeated low-effort spam or off-topic promotion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to the maintainers through the project organization contact channel. If the issue is also security-sensitive, follow `SECURITY.md`.
